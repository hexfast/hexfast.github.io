Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2025

HEXFAST ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how our application ("the App") handles user data.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, log, sell, or share any of the following:

  • Browsing history
  • Traffic content
  • DNS queries
  • IP addresses
  • Connection timestamps
  • Bandwidth usage linked to an individual
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

The App is designed to operate without requiring user accounts or personal identification.

2. Limited Technical Information

The App may temporarily process non-identifiable technical information solely for the purpose of:

  • Establishing and maintaining network connections
  • Diagnosing connection failures
  • Improving app stability and performance

This information is processed locally on the device and is not persisted or associated with any individual user.

3. Payments

Payments and subscriptions are handled exclusively through Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not receive or store any payment credentials or billing information.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at:
