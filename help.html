Help Center

Last updated: May 21, 2026

This page answers common questions about HEXFAST. It is intended to help users quickly understand how the app works and how to complete basic setup.

1. What is HEXFAST?

HEXFAST is a VPN utility app that helps users create a private network connection.

2. Do I need to create an account?

No. HEXFAST does not require a separate username or password. The app creates an anonymous app session automatically when you launch it.

3. How do I start using the app?

  • Open the app.
  • Go to the Plans tab.
  • Select a subscription plan and complete the Apple in-app purchase flow.
  • Return to the Home tab.
  • Select a server if needed and tap the power button.
  • Allow the iOS VPN permission prompt when requested.

4. How are subscriptions handled?

Subscriptions are provided through Apple's In-App Purchase system. In TestFlight and App Review, purchases use Apple's sandbox environment and do not create real charges.

5. How do I restore purchases?

Open the More tab and tap Restore Purchases. This asks Apple to re-check your subscription status for the current Apple Account.

6. Why might plans or subscription status take a moment to appear?

The app may need a few seconds to refresh product information, restore subscription state, or load server information after launch or after a purchase. If needed, wait briefly and try again.

7. Why might I be unable to connect right away?

  • Your subscription may not be active yet.
  • The app may still be loading server information.
  • The VPN permission prompt may not have been accepted yet.
  • Your network may be temporarily unavailable.

8. Does the app require a reviewer login?

No reviewer account, username, or password is required to open or evaluate the app.

9. Does HEXFAST collect browsing history or VPN traffic content?

No. HEXFAST does not sell, use, or disclose VPN traffic data, browsing history, or DNS query contents. More information is available in our Privacy Policy.

10. Where can I get support?

If you need help, contact admin@hexfast.com.
